Certifications and Standards

Every diamond above 0.30 carats at Kirthi Diamonds is certified by the Gemological Institute of America (GIA) or the International Gemological Institute (IGI) , ensuring internationally recognised grading for cut, colour, clarity, and carat weight. All gold jewellery is BIS Hallmarked in 18kt and 22kt configurations, guaranteeing purity in accordance with the Bureau of Indian Standards.
